Title: Trim low-signal resume/site content (minimal edits only)

## Problem
Some resume or website copy may dilute senior-level signal by adding noise, repetition, or dated details.

## Goal
Make minimal edits that remove weak content and improve clarity without redesigning the site.

## Scope
- Review hero, resume summary, and obvious low-value sections
- Remove or tighten weak bullets and repetitive copy
- Avoid structural rewrites unless necessary

## Acceptance Criteria
- Low-signal content is reduced with minimal code churn
- Important experience remains intact
- Edits improve readability and senior positioning
- No large design or layout changes are introduced

## Notes for Copilot coding agent
Treat this as editorial cleanup. Prefer deletion and tightening over new content generation.
